Abstract.

Introduction

The ODLR Profile for Terms of Service Language (TOSL) enhances the understanding and enforcement of legal terms within service agreements. Utilizing the Open Digital Rights Language (ODLR), this profile specifies the obligations, rights, and prohibitions contained in agreements to effectively identify and flag potentially unfair terms. By integrating ODLR's flexible framework, the TSL profile aims to ensure clearer, more enforceable, and fairer terms of service agreements across diverse digital platforms.

"This ontology and the tool ReSpecter is still in active development. See ReSpecter for more details on the tool."
